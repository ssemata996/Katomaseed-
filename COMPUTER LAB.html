  COMPUTER LABORATORY

Katoma seed secondary school has a dedicated space designed for use of computers and other related technology. Laboratory is typically used for research and educational services. Arrange of computer in our lab are equipped with necessary hardware and software for education or research purposes. There is a high-speed internet connection for research, communication and accessing online resources available in our lab. Our laboratory is equipped with printing facilities for processing document and the network infrastructures for example routers, switch and the other networking equipment to support multiple user and ensure connectivity school wide.

Our computer laboratory provides students with access to technology for learning purposes such as research, coding and data analysis.

The Lab supports academic and scientific research that requires specialized software or powerful computing resources, all are available in our school computer laboratory.
