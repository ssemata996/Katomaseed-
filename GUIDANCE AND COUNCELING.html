At Katoma seed secondary school, we believe that every student deserves support to achieve their academic, personal and social goals. Our school counseling and guidance program is designed to provide students with a safe, nurturing an inclusive environment to explore their interests, address challenges and develop essential life skills.

HOW DO GUIDANCE AND COUNSELLING AT OUR SCHOOL WORK?

Our mission is to provide comprehensive counseling and guidance services that promote students success, well-being and character development. We strive to foster a collaborative relationship between students, parents, teachers and the community to support the unique needs of each student.  

Guidance and counseling determines the interests, intelligence, attitude, personality and abilities of our students. Counselors work with individuals to make desirable adjustments and any particular area of experience. Our guidance providers stay updated and possess knowledge of future study possibities and other relevant factors. Thus they support students in exploring their own options.

The general importance of guidance and counseling is that they provide services that promote personal, social, educational and future development of our students. It also helps to remove undesirables’ traits by recognizing ones talent and abilities and also help an optimistic outlook. They also aid in developing resourcefulness and self-direction in adopting the changes in the society.
