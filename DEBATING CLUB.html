DEBATING CLUB

The debating club at Katoma seed is vibrant and dynamic group of students who share a passion for public speaking , argumentation and critical thinking. Our  club provides  a platform ,build confidence and engage in international discussions 

OBJECTIVES

  1. Developing confidence and effectiveness in communicating ideas and aguements.

  2. analyse coplex topics, evaluate evidence and develop well supported arguements.

  3. work with fellow club members to research, prepare and present debates .

  4. explore and discuss current events, social issues and political topics .
