ABOUT US

Katoma seed secondary school is a Ugandan secondary school in Katoma, Kajjere Kiboga district Uganda. It is registered as a partnership school by the Ministry of Education and Sports (MoES). It started in 1994 but with very few students, now it is among the best. We cannot forget to thank parents and teachers who made the school (education) more realistic.

Katoma seed secondary school is for boys and girls and offers "O"&"A" level, mixed day and boarding. Our mission is to provide all round quality education aimed at producing innovative, creative and responsible citizens global wide.

Our school has teachers who are composed even in challenging situations. They understand that students learn at different pase and provide additional support when needed. Teachers of Katoma seed secondary school are qualified and able to analyze what work best for their students and the more between different learning theories and teaching methodologies. Our teachers communicate effective with students and collaborate with other teachers, administration, parents and entire communities.

At Katoma seed secondary school, we believe that in nurturing not only knowledgeable students but well-rounded individuals and we envision a community where every student feel valued, included and encouraged to achieve excellence.
