Art club

Art club is a vibrant community of artists, creatives and art enthusiasts who share a passion for visual arts. Our mission is to provide a supportive and inspiring environment where members can explore their creativity, develop their artistic skills and showcase their work.

Our objectives

Provide opportunities for members to express themselves through various art forms such as painting, drawing scriptures and photography.

Offer workshop, tutorials and critiques to help members improve their autistic techniques and styles.

Organize exhibitions, display and showcases to promote members art work and provide feedback from peers and the professionals.

Collaborate with local art organization, galleries and businesses to engage with the broader art community.

Our activities

Host workshop on various art techniques such as painting, drawings and printmaking.

Organize exhibitions and showcase to display members art work.

Provide constructive feedback and critiques to help members improve their artistic skills.

Organize field trips to local art galleries, museum and studios.

Join Katoma seed School kiboga

If you are passionate about art creative and self-expression, we invite you to join our community. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced artist, we welcome you to share talents and inspire others.
