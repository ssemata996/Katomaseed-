Writers club

The writer’s club is a community of writers, authors and literacy enthusiasts who share a passion for creative writing, storytelling and safe expression. Our mission is to provide a supportive and inspiring environment where writers can develop their craft, share their work and connect with like-minded individuals.

Our vision

To Foster a vibrant community of writers who inspire, motivate Andy support each other in their writing journey.

Objectives

Provide opportunities for students to develop their craft through workshop, writing exercises and constructive feedbacks.

Create a platform for students to share their work, receive feedback and learn from each other.

 Foster is supportive and inclusive community and where the students can connect collaborate and inspire each other.

Organize events, reading and discussions to promote literacy awareness and applications.

Our activities

Host workshop on various topics such as nonfiction or fiction, poetry and screenwriting.

Facilitate writing group we are members can share their work, receive feedback and support each other.

 Organize reading and literacy festivals to promote literacy awareness and appreciation.

Join our vibrant community of learning.
