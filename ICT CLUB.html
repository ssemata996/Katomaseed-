ICT CLUB


The ICT club is a vibrant  community of students passionate about technology and innovation. Our club provides a platform for students  to explore their interests in informationa and communication technology, develop their skills and work on exciting projects.
Our objectives are ; encourage students to explore the world of ICT and   develop  a pasion for innovation ,provide students with hands on experience in coding, programming, website design and other ICT related areas.
Encourage students to work together on projects, share ideas and learn from others
Equip students with a skill and knowledge require to persue career in the ICT industry.
MEMBERSHIP

The ICT club is open to all students at KATOMA SEED regardless of their skills level or experience. Whether you are a beginner or an experienct ICT enthusiast, we welcome you to join our community.
