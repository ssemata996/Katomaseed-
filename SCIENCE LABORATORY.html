SCIENCE LABORATORY

Our science laboratory has a variety of equipments, materials and safety features that facilitate experiments and hands-on-learning in subjects like a biology, chemistry and physics.

Equipments

1.  Laboratory benches: there is a durable workstation for conducting experiments.

2.  Fume hoods: our lab is well ventilated exposure that protects students from exposure to hazardous fumes during some experiments.

3.  Bunsen burners: they're available for heating substances during scientific investigations.

4.  Glassware: these include; beakers (various sizes), test tubes and rackers, graduated cylinders, Petri dishes and enlenmey flasks among others.

5.  Balance scale: for measuring Mass

6.  Microscopes: for examining small specimens.

7.  Hotplates: for gentle heating of samples

8.  Water bath: for maintaining samples at specific temperature

9.  Thermometers: for measuring temperature

10.             PH meters: for measuring acidity, neutral or alkalinity.

Among others

Supplies

1.  Chemicals: a variety of reagents, acids, base and indicators for experiments all are available in our school science laboratory enhance facilitating scientific investigations.

2.  Biological specimen: slides prepared for spaceman or model for biological studies also in our lab.

3.  Safety gears for example laboratory coats, safety goggles, gloves and face shield among others
