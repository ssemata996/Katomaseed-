Entrepreneurship club

 

Entrepreneurship club is a community of passionate and driven individuals who share common interests in entrepreneurship and innovation. Our mission is to provide a platform for members to connect, learn and grow while fostering a culture of entrepreneurship and creativity.

Our vision

To be the leading entrepreneurship club at Katoma seed School kiboga, providing a supportive ecosystem for entrepreneurs to turn their ideas into reality.

Our objectives

Encourage and support the members in developing their entrepreneurship skills and Mindy set.

Offer access resources and networking opportunities to help members you grow their venture.

Encourage members to think creatively and develop initiative solution to real world problems.

Create a supportive and inclusive community where members can share their experience, learn from each other and grow together.

Our activities

Organize workshop and the seminars in various topics related to entrepreneurship such as business planning, marketing and fundraising.

Host pitch competition where members can showcase their business ideas and compete for prizes.

Organizing networking events that bring together members, entrepreneurs and industry professionals in our community.

Join us

If you are passionate about entrepreneurship and innovation, we invite you to join our community. Whether a student or simply someone who wants to make a difference, we welcome you to be part of our journey of fulfilling the demands of 21st century full of development via entrepreneurship and technology.
