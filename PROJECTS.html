                        PROJECTS            

Life skills are essential competences that enable individual to effectively navigate the challenges and complexities of daily life. They encompass a wide range of abilities including critical thinking, problem solving, communication, decision making, self awareness and interpersonal skills. Recognizing the significance of life skills in the holistic development of our students, we propose projects to strengthen life skills education in our school curriculum.

The goals of these projects are recommended by the Uganda National Examination Board (UNEB) to enhance the integration and effectiveness of life skills education within the school curriculum. By equipping our students with essential life skills, we aim to empower them to become responsible and successful individuals in their personal, academics and professional lives.

Our school collaborates with curriculum experts, educators and stakeholders to develop guide lines and recommendations for the inclusion of life skills in various subjects and grade levels.

Our teachers are trained and have ability to ensure successful implementation of the enhanced curriculum by developing a range of high quality resources including assessment tools, project plans to support our students in engaging and interacting life skills education.

Our teachers are to develop a wide range of life skills that will enable them to navigate challenges, make informed decisions, communicate effectively and develop strong interpersonal relationships as this will contribute to their personal growth, academic success and future employability.
