HEALTH SERVICES

At Katoma seed secondary school, we prioritize the health well-being, safety of our students, staff and the community. Our school health services   aims to provide accessible, confidential and comprehensive care to support the unique needs of each student.

Our goals of school health services is to prepare the younger generation to adopt measures to remain healthy so as to help them to make the best use of educational facilities, to utilize leisure in productive and constructive manner.

 

 

To promote healthy life style, prevent illness and provide early intervention for health concerns.

To help our students become healthy and useful citizens who will be able to perform their role effectively for the welfare of themselves and their families and the community at large and worldwide.

“We call upon parents to work hand in hand with the school such that the health of our children is achieved to ensure a successful life.”
